## Serbian Dominance Before EuroBasket

In the final test before EuroBasket, Serbia proved to be the main contender for the title. With a convincing triumph over Slovenia (106–72), the national team sent a clear signal to all of Europe.

## Game Overview

From start to finish, Serbia controlled the tempo, while Slovenia looked dangerous only during those ten minutes of Dončić's first quarter. As their shooting rhythm faded, the deficit grew, and eventually Dončić went to the bench while Serbia celebrated with a full roster.

## Individual Performance

### Serbia:

- Nikola Jović: 18 points and 7 rebounds
- Bogdan Bogdanović: 14 points with an impressive 4/5 from three-point range
- Nikola Jokić: 10 points, 8 rebounds, and 4 assists

### Slovenia:

- Luka Dončić: 17 points, 7 rebounds, 4 assists
